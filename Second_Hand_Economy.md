# Second-Hand Economy

## Overview

This page documents the secondary marketplace and player-to-player trading economy for EV2 assets. The secondary market is a **core pillar of EV2's economic design**, enabling price discovery, asset redistribution, and creating continuous economic value for players of all engagement levels. EV2 actively encourages and facilitates P2P exchange through multiple integrated marketplaces.

---

## Multi-Platform Trading Ecosystem

### EV2's Marketplace Philosophy

EV2 embraces an **open, decentralized trading ecosystem** rather than restricting players to a single platform:

- **Player Autonomy**: Users trade where they choose with maximum flexibility
- **Network Effects**: Multiple marketplaces create liquidity and price discovery
- **No Platform Lock-in**: Assets fully portable across all trading venues
- **Competition Benefits Players**: Multiple platforms drive better UX and lower fees
- **Blockchain Native**: Direct wallet-to-wallet transfers, true asset ownership

### Trading Venues

Players can trade EV2 assets across multiple integrated platforms:

#### In-Game Marketplace

Direct trading integrated into game client:

- **Built-In Exchange**: Buy/sell NFTs without leaving game
- **Premium Currency Integration**: Purchase using in-game premium currency
- **Token Support**: Accept any blockchain token (ETH, USDC, native tokens, etc.)
- **Seamless UX**: Trading experience optimized for gaming
- **Real-Time Pricing**: Live market data displayed in-game
- **Escrow System**: Smart contract ensures fair transactions
- **User-Friendly Interface**: Non-technical players can trade easily
- **Tax/Fee Structure**: Transparent fees displayed before purchase

#### OpenSea Integration

Trading on industry-leading NFT marketplace:

- **Maximum Exposure**: Reach largest NFT audience
- **Community Tools**: Advanced filtering, bidding, collections
- **Price History**: Full analytics and trading data
- **Verified Listings**: Community reviews and trust signals
- **Wallet Direct**: Connect any Web3 wallet
- **Collection Pages**: Official EV2 collections with verification
- **External Liquidity**: Traders from across Web3 ecosystem

#### Funtico Website Marketplace

Dedicated marketplace hosted by publisher:

- **Official Platform**: First-party trading hub for EV2 assets
- **Integrated Features**: Game integration and exclusive tools
- **Player Profiles**: Community showcase and reputation system
- **Trading Analytics**: Charts, trends, and price discovery
- **Event Exclusives**: Special limited items available only here
- **Community Features**: Forums, guides, trading discussion

#### Community/Third-Party Platforms

Support for emerging trading solutions:

- **X2Y2, Blur, Magic Eden**: Any platform supporting Ethereum/blockchain assets
- **Future Platforms**: New marketplaces emerge as Web3 evolves
- **Player Choice**: Freedom to trade on preferred platform
- **Interoperability**: Assets work on any platform supporting standards
- **Decentralized**: No single platform gatekeeps all trading

### Asset Liquidity

Different NFT types have varying market characteristics based on utility and scarcity:

#### High-Liquidity Assets

Constantly traded with tight price ranges:

- **Cosmetics (Chromas)**: High volume, stable pricing, large audience
- **Common Pilots/Weapons**: Beginners constantly buying, veterans selling
- **Standard Access Tokens**: Regular demand from new players
- **Crafting Materials**: Ongoing need for blueprints conversion

#### Medium-Liquidity Assets

Regular trading with moderate price variation:

- **Rare Pilots**: Specialist builds sought by enthusiasts
- **Epic Weapons**: Leveled weapons with good synergies
- **Legendary Artifacts**: Specific modifier combinations valuable
- **Guild Tokens**: Periodic new member acquisition

#### High-Value, Lower-Liquidity Assets

Rare finds with significant price discovery:

- **Exotic Weapons**: Exceptionally rare, high collectibility
- **Legendary Artifacts**: High-level, unique modifications
- **Legacy Items**: Event cosmetics no longer available
- **Perfect Stat Combination Pilots**: Specific feat/weakness synergies
- **Fully Leveled Endgame Gear**: Represents massive player investment

## In-Game Marketplace Integration

### Seamless Trading Experience

EV2 integrates NFT trading directly into the game client:

#### Trading Interface

- **Inventory Access**: Browse owned NFTs directly from game menu
- **Listing Management**: Create/edit/cancel listings without leaving game
- **Purchase Flow**: Complete transaction entirely in-game
- **Real-Time Notifications**: Receive alerts when items sell or bids arrive
- **Price Charts**: Historical pricing and trend data visible in-game
- **Rarity Filters**: Search by stat combinations, rarity, level
- **Wishlist System**: Mark desired items for price tracking

#### Premium Currency Support

In-game marketplace accepts multiple payment methods:

- **Native Premium Currency**: Direct in-game currency purchases (e.g., "Gold")
- **Multiple Blockchain Tokens**: Accept ETH, USDC, USDT, etc.
- **Stablecoin Preferred**: Reduce volatility for pricing
- **Wallet Integration**: Non-custodial transactions from player wallets
- **Conversion Rates**: Exchange rates clearly displayed before purchase
- **Gas Fee Transparency**: Show network costs before committing

#### Transaction Security

- **Smart Contract Escrow**: Funds held securely during transaction
- **Dispute Resolution**: In-game arbitration for transaction issues
- **Verified Sellers**: Reputation system indicates trustworthy traders
- **Anti-Fraud Measures**: Prevents common scams and exploitation
- **Transaction Reversibility**: Limited reversal window for buyer protection

#### Fee Structure

Transparent, player-friendly fee system:

- **Seller Fees**: Small commission when listing sells (e.g., 3-5%)
- **Buyer Fees**: Optional minimal buyer fees (if any)
- **Royalties**: Original creator receives small percentage per resale
- **Network Costs**: Gas fees covered or subsidized by platform
- **Fee Disclosure**: Complete breakdown shown before transaction
- *Specific fee percentages to be determined by game operator*

### Cross-Platform Benefits

In-game marketplace connected to broader ecosystem:

- **Unified Inventory**: See all NFTs across all platforms
- **Price Synchronization**: In-game prices reflect market averages
- **Arbitrage Prevention**: Prices balanced across marketplaces
- **Liquidity Aggregation**: Draw from all trading pools
- **Seamless Transfers**: Move items between platforms easily

## Trading Mechanics & Features

### Listing Types

Different trading approaches available to players:

#### Direct Sale Listings

Fixed-price items for immediate purchase:

- **Asking Price**: Seller sets specific price
- **Instant Purchase**: Buyer purchases immediately at listed price
- **No Negotiation**: Take-it-or-leave-it offer
- **Quick Sales**: Fast execution for sellers
- **Price Certainty**: Buyers know exact cost upfront

#### Auction System

Competitive bidding for valuable items:

- **Opening Bid**: Seller sets minimum acceptable price
- **Bid Increments**: Minimum increase between bids
- **Duration**: Set time window for bidding (24-72 hours typical)
- **Automatic Extension**: Extend if bid received near end time
- **Bidder Protection**: No surprise price jumps at auction end
- **Winner Notification**: Automatic communication of winning bid

#### Offer System

Buyers make offers on listed items:

- **Below-Asking Offers**: Propose price lower than listing
- **Negotiation**: Back-and-forth price discussion
- **Seller Discretion**: Accept/reject/counter-offer
- **Time-Limited Offers**: Offers expire to prevent stagnation
- **Bulk Offers**: Buyers offer for multiple items at discount

#### Collection Offers

Buyers bid on entire collection categories:

- **Rarity-Based**: "I'll buy any Rare Weapon for up to X price"
- **Stats-Based**: "I'll pay X for weapons with 80+ accuracy"
- **Level-Based**: "Buy all level 20+ pilots at standing offer"
- **Convenience**: Sellers auto-accept qualifying items
- **Quick Offloading**: Rapidly liquidate collections

### Price Discovery Mechanisms

Market naturally discovers fair prices:

- **Supply & Demand**: Limited exotic items naturally higher priced
- **Speculation**: Traders speculate on item demand
- **Information Efficiency**: Market prices reflect all known information
- **Competition**: Multiple marketplaces prevent price fixing
- **Transparency**: All prices public, preventing hidden deals
- **Historical Data**: Complete trading history available for analysis

### Secondary Market Valuation

What drives NFT prices on secondary market:

#### Mech Pilots Pricing

- **Base Stat Quality**: Rarer stat combinations command premiums
- **Pilot Level**: High-level pilots worth exponentially more
- **Feat Synergies**: Specific feat combinations valuable for specific builds
- **Specialization Demand**: Hot builds currently in meta worth more
- **Rarity Tier**: Exotic pilots always premium
- **Age**: Legacy pilots from early seasons command collectible premiums

#### Weapon Pricing

- **Rarity Tier**: Exotic weapons highest value
- **Base Stats**: Perfect stat rolls extremely valuable
- **Modification Progress**: Fully unlocked modifications increase value
- **Weapon Level**: Leveled weapons worth 3-5x fresh weapons
- **Unique Modifiers**: Weapons with rare modifier combinations premium
- **Meta Relevance**: Currently-competitive builds worth more
- **Historical Significance**: Legacy weapons from limited events appreciated

#### Artifact Pricing

- **Modification Type**: Support/rare modifications more valuable
- **Stat Quality**: Flat +% bonuses valued for consistency
- **Artifact Level**: Leveled artifacts exponentially more valuable
- **Synergy Potential**: Artifacts enabling new builds worth premiums
- **Rarity**: Legendary/Exotic always command high prices
- **Use Case Popularity**: Currently-meta builds drive demand

#### Cosmetic (Chroma) Pricing

- **Limited Availability**: Event cosmetics become rarer over time
- **Exclusivity**: Cosmetics no longer obtainable spike in value
- **Aesthetic Appeal**: Community-favored cosmetics worth more
- **Collectibility**: Complete sets drive demand for individual items
- **Cultural Significance**: National pride cosmetics culturally valuable
- **Sponsorship Legacy**: Expired partner cosmetics become collectible
- **Age**: Older cosmetics more valuable (legacy status)

#### Guild Token Pricing

- **Guild Reputation**: Successful guilds' tokens worth more
- **Guild Level**: Progression tier determines token value
- **Active Membership**: Guilds with active membership more valuable
- **Treasury Size**: Guilds with substantial funds worth more
- **War Effort Progress**: Guilds near content unlock worth premiums
- **Tournament History**: Successful guild records increase value

#### Access Token Pricing

- **Remaining Uses**: More remaining uses = higher price
- **Progression Unlocked**: Tokens with unlocked tiers worth more
- **Time Remaining**: Tokens near expiration discounted
- **Content Popularity**: Access to sought-after content worth more
- **Exclusivity**: Rare access tokens naturally premium

## Economic Health & Market Balance

### Anti-Inflation Mechanisms

Game economy designed to prevent runaway inflation:

- **Loot Sinks**: High-end content requires resource consumption
- **Crafting Costs**: NFT minting requires spending currency
- **Cosmetic Purchases**: Players spend on account-bound cosmetics
- **Guild Taxation**: Guilds tax members removing currency from circulation
- **Content Gating**: Expensive raids consume resources
- **Seasonal Resets**: Economy rebalanced each season

### Market Transparency

Prevent hidden manipulation and maintain trust:

- **All Trades Public**: Complete trade history transparent on blockchain
- **Price Oracles**: Third-party price feeds available
- **Wash Trade Prevention**: Detect suspicious same-wallet buying/selling
- **Market Manipulation Rules**: Enforced against obvious manipulation
- **Community Monitoring**: Players identify suspicious trading
- **Dispute Resolution**: Arbitration for contested transactions

### Player Protection

Safeguards against exploitation:

- **Non-Custodial Ownership**: Players directly own all assets
- **Wallet Security**: No platform can seize assets
- **Insurance Considerations**: *Optional platform insurance to be determined*
- **Scam Education**: Resources teaching players about common scams
- **Verified Sellers**: Badge system for trustworthy traders
- **Fair Pricing**: Tools helping players identify fair market rates

### Wealth Redistribution

Secondary market creates natural wealth distribution:

- **Earning Path**: F2P players sell excess loot to wealthier players
- **Income Generation**: Active traders earn from arbitrage and flipping
- **Democratized Access**: Secondary market makes premium items accessible
- **Social Mobility**: Skilled players outcompete wealthy players
- **Engagement Incentive**: Trading itself becomes engaging content
- **Community Building**: Traders become invested community members

## Gaming the Economy (Intentionally)

### Professions & Specializations

Players develop trading specialties:

#### Flippers

Buy underpriced assets, resell at market rate:

- **Skill**: Identify mispriced items
- **Capital**: Requires holdings for acquisition
- **Risk**: Price movements against position
- **Reward**: Profit from price inefficiencies
- **Community Role**: Provide liquidity, improve price discovery

#### Collectors

Assemble valuable collections:

- **Focus**: Complete sets of specific items
- **Strategy**: Hunt missing pieces
- **Patience**: Wait for good prices on specific items
- **Value**: Collections worth more than sum of parts
- **Status**: Rare collections display prestige

#### Grinders

Farm rare items through gameplay:

- **Playtime**: Investment of gameplay hours
- **Efficiency**: Optimize farming routes and techniques
- **Inventory**: Store valuable drops for sale
- **Market Timing**: Sell when prices peak
- **Income**: Steady passive income from farming

#### Content Creators

Use market analysis for content:

- **Videos**: Market reviews, price predictions, flipping guides
- **Streams**: Trading streams, market analysis
- **Guides**: Written guides to valuation and trading
- **Audience**: Build community around trading
- **Revenue**: Multiple monetization from audience engagement

### Economy Profession Incentives

- All playstyles generate revenue from trading
- Active players earn more than passive players
- Trading creates engagement beyond combat
- Community around trading builds retention
- Natural skill-based economic hierarchy

## Preventing Toxicity & Scams

### Common Scams to Prevent

#### Pump & Dump Schemes

- **Social Manipulation**: Hype item artificially to pump price
- **Distribution**: Organized groups buy at low price
- **Promotion**: Mislead players into buying at peak
- **Exit**: Orchestrated dumping drives price down
- **Prevention**: Community education, price monitoring

#### Rugpull Prevention

- **Asset Security**: All assets secured on blockchain
- **No Platform Risk**: No central party can rug
- **Smart Contract Audits**: Published contract audits
- **Transparency**: Clear development roadmap
- **Community Oversight**: Active player community monitoring

#### Impersonation Scams

- **Fake Official Accounts**: Impersonators claiming to be developers
- **Phishing**: Fake marketplace links stealing wallets
- **Discord Scams**: Fraudulent "admin" accounts in communities
- **Prevention**: Verified badges, security education, active moderation

#### Price Fixing

- **Collusion Detection**: Identify organized price manipulation
- **Circuit Breakers**: Automatic trading halts on extreme volatility
- **Enforcement**: Action against detected manipulation
- **Community Reporting**: Players report suspicious activity
- **Transparency**: Public monitoring of organized trading

### Community Safety Features

- **Verified Badges**: Official accounts marked
- **Scam Reporting**: Easy reporting mechanism
- **Education Resources**: Guides teaching safe trading
- **Moderation**: Active community management
- **Terms of Service**: Clear rules against manipulation
- **Enforcement**: Consequences for violation
