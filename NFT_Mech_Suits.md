# Mech Suits NFT

## Overview

Mech Suits are non-purchasable, tradeable NFTs earned through gameplay. Each suit is a unique piece of equipment that serves as the physical platform for pilots, determining combat capabilities, carrying capacity, and special abilities. Suits range dramatically in power and utility, creating a diverse equipment ecosystem where players constantly hunt for upgrades and specialized builds.

---

## Suit Statistics & Attributes

### Armor & Defense

Each suit possesses defensive characteristics:

- **Base Armor**: Damage reduction percentage against incoming fire
- **Health Pool**: Total durability before suit destruction
- **Armor Type**: Different materials provide varying resistances
  - Kinetic armor (bullets, missiles)
  - Energy armor (lasers, plasma)
  - Elemental armor (fire, ice, shock)
- **Recovery Mechanics**: Some suits regenerate armor over time

### Carrying Capacity

Suits have distinct inventory limitations:

- **Ammo Slots**: Determines how much ammunition can be carried
  - Different weapon types require different ammo allocations
  - Heavier weapons consume more ammo capacity
  - Balance between firepower and sustainability
- **Inventory Slots**: General storage for consumables, components, modules
  - Med kits, grenades, gadgets
  - Crafting materials and upgrade components
  - Equipment modifications and enhancements
- **Weight Class**: Heavier suits carry more but move slower
- **Specialization Trade-offs**: Tank suits sacrifice speed for capacity; light suits sacrifice carrying for mobility

## Equipment System

### Weapon Slots

Suits have specific hardpoints for weapon mounting:

- **Primary Weapon Slot**: Main firearm (assault rifle, sniper, cannon, etc.)
- **Secondary Weapon Slot**: Backup weapon for different scenarios
- **Melee Slot**: Integrated close-combat weapon
- **Utility Slot**: Non-lethal tool (taser, EMP, smoke grenades)
- **Compatibility Requirements**: Only certain weapons fit specific hardpoints
- **Tactical Flexibility**: Swap loadouts between missions

### Weapon Synergies

Some suits have special bonuses for specific weapon types:

- **Ballistics Specialization**: Bonuses to bullet-based weapons
- **Energy Weapons Proficiency**: Improved laser/plasma effectiveness
- **Explosive Expertise**: Better grenade and missile handling
- **Melee Augmentation**: Enhanced close-combat damage
- **Dual-Wielding Support**: Special suits for weapon combinations

## Unique Suit Features

### Passive Abilities

Every suit has inherent unique characteristics:

- **Regeneration**: Automatically restore armor over time
- **Kinetic Absorption**: Reduce knockback from explosions
- **Stealth Field**: Reduced detection range by enemies
- **Overheating Dissipation**: Weapons cool faster
- **Energy Efficiency**: Special weapons consume less power
- **Ammunition Scavenging**: Find additional ammo from defeated enemies
- **Structural Integrity**: Resist status effects (stun, freeze, burn)

### Unique Mechanics

Special suit characteristics that differentiate playstyles:

- **Shield Generators**: Deploy temporary energy barriers
- **Movement Augmentation**: Specialized thrust for advanced maneuvers
- **Cloaking Phases**: Brief periods of invisibility
- **Damage Resistance**: Suit automatically reduces specific damage types
- **Loot Attraction**: Increase rare loot drop rates in vicinity
- **Ally Buffs**: Grant bonuses to nearby teammates

## Activated Skills & Abilities

Inspired by Anthem's suit-specific powers, each suit features timed activated skills:

### Skill Categories

#### Offensive Abilities

Skills designed for damage output:

- **Rocket Barrage**: Launch salvo of homing missiles (cooldown: 20 seconds)
- **Plasma Beam**: Sustained high-damage energy beam (duration: 5 seconds, cooldown: 15 seconds)
- **Explosive Ordinance**: Deploy proximity mines in area (cooldown: 25 seconds)
- **Railgun Strike**: Single massive projectile (cooldown: 30 seconds)
- **Sonic Blast**: Cone attack stunning and damaging enemies (cooldown: 18 seconds)

#### Defensive Abilities

Skills for protection and survivability:

- **Shield Dome**: Create protective bubble around suit (duration: 8 seconds, cooldown: 20 seconds)
- **Temporal Slow**: Freeze time in area, slowing enemies (duration: 4 seconds, cooldown: 25 seconds)
- **Damage Reflection**: Redirect incoming damage back to attackers (duration: 6 seconds, cooldown: 22 seconds)
- **Healing Surge**: Restore significant armor health (cooldown: 15 seconds)
- **Invulnerability Frame**: Brief period of complete protection (duration: 2 seconds, cooldown: 30 seconds)

#### Mobility Abilities

Skills for movement and positioning:

- **Teleport**: Instantly move to targeted location (cooldown: 20 seconds)
- **Boost Dash**: Perform high-speed directional rush (cooldown: 12 seconds)
- **Vertical Ascent**: Rapid altitude gain for positioning (cooldown: 15 seconds)
- **Hover Mode**: Float in place for precise aiming (duration: 8 seconds, cooldown: 18 seconds)
- **Dash Network**: Chain multiple dash teleports (cooldown: 25 seconds)

#### Support Abilities

Skills for team assistance:

- **Team Heal**: Restore nearby allies' suit integrity (cooldown: 20 seconds)
- **Damage Amplification**: Buff nearby allies' damage output (duration: 10 seconds, cooldown: 25 seconds)
- **Speed Boost**: Grant movement speed buff to teammates (duration: 8 seconds, cooldown: 18 seconds)
- **Revive Protocol**: Remotely restore downed teammate (cooldown: 30 seconds)
- **Tactical Beacon**: Mark enemy weak points for team (duration: 15 seconds, cooldown: 20 seconds)

### Ability Mechanics

- **Cooldown System**: Fixed recharge time between uses
- **Energy Cost**: Some abilities consume suit energy (limits spam usage)
- **Skill Expression**: Timing and positioning critical for effectiveness
- **Combo Potential**: Abilities combine with pilot feats for powerful synergies
- **Loadout Flexibility**: Different missions require different ability selections

## Suit Specialization & Class Diversity

### Tank Suits

Heavy defensive platforms:

- **High Armor & Health**: Absorb massive damage
- **Large Ammo Capacity**: Sustained suppressive fire
- **Defensive Abilities**: Shield, reflection, damage reduction
- **Slow Movement**: Trade-off for durability
- **Team Protection**: Block and absorb damage for allies

### Assault Suits

Balanced offensive platforms:

- **Moderate Armor**: Decent defense with room for offense
- **Weapon Versatility**: Strong across multiple weapon types
- **Offensive Abilities**: Missiles, beams, explosives
- **Standard Mobility**: Balanced speed and maneuverability
- **Consistent Performance**: Reliable in all mission types

### Scout Suits

Light and mobile platforms:

- **Low Armor**: Minimal defense
- **Small Ammo Capacity**: Precision over firepower
- **Mobility Abilities**: Teleport, dash, stealth
- **High Speed**: Superior agility
- **Hit-and-Run Tactics**: Strike then retreat gameplay

### Support Suits

Utility-focused platforms:

- **Moderate Stats**: Balanced across categories
- **Support Abilities**: Heals, buffs, revives
- **Inventory Focus**: Carry consumables and equipment
- **Team Synergies**: Abilities enhance allies
- **Resource Management**: Enable squad persistence

### Specialist Suits

Unique niche platforms:

- **Unique Mechanics**: Unconventional gameplay mechanics
- **Special Features**: Only available on specific suits
- **Mission-Specific Strength**: Shine in particular scenarios
- **Skill Expression**: High ceiling for expert players
- **Collectible Appeal**: Legendary or exotic rarity

## Suit Progression & Upgrades

### Modification System

Suits can be enhanced over time:

- **Armor Upgrades**: Increase defensive stats
- **Weapon Slot Expansion**: Add additional hardpoints
- **Ability Enhancement**: Improve cooldowns and duration
- **Module Installation**: Add passive abilities and perks
- **Cosmetic Customization**: Apply [NFT_Chroma_Skins](NFT_Chroma_Skins.md) for visual identity

### Leveling Through Use

Suits gain experience from combat:

- **Performance Experience**: Every mission generates suit XP
- **Combat Statistics**: Kills, defeats, objectives contribute XP
- **Level Progression**: Suits gradually improve stats
- **Permanent Advancement**: Leveling never resets
- **Secondary Market Value**: Leveled suits more valuable (see [Second-Hand Economy](Second-Hand Economy.md))

## Suit Acquisition

### Gameplay Earning

Mech Suits are exclusively earned through gameplay:

- **Mission Rewards**: Defeat enemies and complete objectives
- **Boss Drops**: Challenging encounters grant powerful suits
- **Loot Chests**: Hidden chests contain rare equipment
- **Event Rewards**: Limited-time events distribute unique suits
- **Challenge Completion**: Difficult tasks unlock special suits
- **Random Drops**: Enemies occasionally drop suit blueprints to craft

### Crafting System

Players can craft suits from components:

- **Component Collection**: Gather parts from gameplay
- **Blueprint Requirements**: Learn blueprint from drops or events
- **Resource Investment**: Craft using collected materials
- **Suit Customization**: Choose color, pattern, or visual variants during crafting
- **Time vs Materials**: Different recipes balance time investment vs resource cost

## NFT Specifications

- **Asset Type**: Non-purchasable, tradeable NFT
- **Acquisition**: Earned exclusively through gameplay
- **Blockchain Verified**: All suit stats and progression recorded on-chain
- **Equipment Persistence**: Remains with suit across transfers
- **Cosmetic Application**: Can be customized with chromas
- **Permanence**: Suit data stored permanently on blockchain
- **Resale**: Can be traded on secondary markets

## Suit Synergy & Team Composition

### Pilot-Suit Chemistry

- Different pilots have feat synergies with specific suits
- Close-range specialist pilot with tank suit = ultimate tank
- Precision expert pilot with scout suit = deadly sniper build
- Support pilot with support suit = unbeatable healer
- Exploration of combinations creates build diversity

### Squad Diversity

- Teams benefit from diverse suit types
- Tank holds line, scouts flank, support sustains
- Specialized suits enable tactical mission strategies
- Casual players can brute force, skilled players optimize
