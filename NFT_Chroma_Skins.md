# Chroma Skins NFT

## Overview

Chroma Skins are purchasable cosmetic NFTs that provide visual customization for mech suits and weapons. Powered by Unreal Engine's advanced material system and material masking, Chromas allow players to express their style and identity without affecting gameplay performance.

---

## Visual Customization System

### Technical Foundation

Chromas utilize **Unreal Engine Materials and Material Masking** to achieve:

- **Complete Visual Rework**: Entire appearance transformation from base design
- **Advanced Color Schemas**: Multiple color channel control and customization
- **Texture Application**: Complex texture overlays and surface finishes
- **Pattern Masking**: Precise application of patterns to specific mech areas
- **Material Effects**: Various finish types (metallic, matte, glossy, weathered, etc.)

### Customizable Elements

- **Color Schemas**: Primary, secondary, and accent colors
- **Textures & Finishes**: Different surface materials (carbon fiber, paint, metal, etc.)
- **Patterns**: Geometric designs, camouflage, or symbolic patterns
- **Decals**: Applied graphics and emblems
- **Regional Symbols**: National flags, military insignia, or cultural iconography

## Chroma Categories

### Patriotic Chromas

Inspired by national pride and cultural identity, these chromas feature country-specific designs:

#### Example: Polish Inspired Chroma

- **Primary Color Schema**: White base with red trim accents
- **National Symbols**: Polish Eagle prominently featured
- **Military Insignia**: Polish Air Force symbols on shoulders
- **Regional Identity**: Allows players to express national pride
- **Variants**: Each nation can have multiple interpretations and designs

#### Other Patriotic Options

- Chromas for all nations and regions
- Customizable with national flags and symbols
- Military branch variations (Air Force, Army, Navy insignia)
- Traditional cultural patterns and motifs

### Camouflage Patterns

Military-inspired tactical camouflage for strategic aesthetics:

#### Standard Camo Variants

- **Jungle Camouflage**: Green and brown foliage patterns
- **Desert Camouflage**: Tan and beige sand-inspired patterns
- **Urban Camouflage**: Gray and black concrete-inspired patterns
- **Night Camouflage**: Dark blue and black low-visibility patterns
- **Arctic Camouflage**: White and light gray snow patterns

#### Tactical Applications

While purely cosmetic, different camo patterns create themed squad aesthetics in multiplayer missions.

### Event-Driven Chromas

Seasonal and limited-time chromas tied to special events and campaigns:

- **Holiday Specials**: Christmas, New Year, seasonal themes
- **Sponsored Campaigns**: Partner brand collaborations
- **Community Events**: Limited editions from tournaments or challenges
- **Temporary Availability**: Creates urgency and collectibility
- **Themed Collections**: Story-driven chromas tied to narrative events

## Gameplay Impact

### Purely Cosmetic

Chroma Skins provide **zero gameplay advantages**:

- ✅ Do not affect pilot stats
- ✅ Do not modify weapon performance
- ✅ Do not provide tactical advantages
- ✅ Do not influence combat mechanics
- ✅ Do not alter hitboxes or detection
- ❌ Not pay-to-win

### Self-Expression & Identity

- Personal aesthetic customization
- Squad coordination through matching chromas
- National or cultural pride representation
- Individual playstyle branding
- Collector appeal through variant hunting

## NFT Specifications

- **Asset Type**: Purchasable NFT (also obtainable via rewards)
- **Tradeable**: Yes (via secondary market)
- **Blockchain Verified**: Ownership and authenticity on-chain
- **Reusable**: Single chroma skin can be applied to multiple suits/weapons
- **Permanence**: Chroma data stored permanently on blockchain
